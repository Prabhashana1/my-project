My Projects

1. Web Application With Spring Boot, Angular and MySQL Database

This web application is developed for a phone repair shop called Mega Supply (Pvt) Ltd. located in Dambulla. This application creates a task related to a phone brought for repair and accepts it to the institution. Then the employees of the company check the phone and update the status related to the task.

✦This application provides the following facilities

  • Creating a repair job.
  • Ability to get sticker labels containing information related to the new job.
  • The customer can check the status of the repair through the QR code on the sticker label.
  • Ability to calculate daily income and monthly income for the owner of the shop.
  • Checking the income of the monthly and daily repairs done by the employees of the shop.
  • Ability to monitor the status of phone that have received for repair.
  • Issuing the bill related to the repair.
  • Notify employees via email when they log in using their account or when someone else tries to log in
